## Universal loop

**Align → Ground → Shape → Act → Verify**

| Step | Key question | Specialist when needed |
|---|---|---|
| Align | What outcome actually matters? | `waza-think`, grill-style clarification |
| Ground | What current evidence constrains us? | `waza-read`, `research-stack` |
| Shape | What is the simplest safe form? | `waza-think`, subagents |
| Act | What small reversible slice moves it forward? | domain-specific skill |
| Verify | What external evidence proves it worked? | `waza-check`, `waza-hunt` |

## Non-negotiables

- Current evidence beats stale memory.
- Ask only when one missing decision blocks safe progress.
- Touch only the requested surface.
- Destructive/irreversible actions need preview or confirmation.
- Output is not done until verified or explicitly marked unverified.
- Persist decisions that must survive restart.
